Getting started as a Household Collector

At almost every junction in our communities, what is expected is a serene environment, instead what catches our attention are heaps of waste at every street corner and these dumps increase daily in spite of enormous amounts spent on collection by authorities. The collection process of these materials are grossly inadequate and non-inclusive of the main party - The Household. Our collectors directly engage and educate these waste producers on our waste to value system/structure, public health and environmental impact

Why choose inCycle?

How to use the app in simple steps

1

Get the app installed, create a profile and choose up to three pickup locations

2

Get the pickup notification within your area of choice and ride to the location

3

Weigh materials received, select materials type received on the app and imput material weight in kilogram

4

Compensation price to be paid is shown, stock the material and approve payment

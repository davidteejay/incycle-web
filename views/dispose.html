How the app works for you

1

DOWNLOAD/SIGNUP

Get a free inCycle app from the apple app store or Google Play on your smartphone and create your account

2

REQUEST PICKUP

When logged in, the app takes your location ehich you manually confirm. Select waste category for disposal. Click Request Pickup. Get matched with your local collector

3

PICKUP

Local collector confirms materials and measures in various categories. He inputs the kilogram/material on his app to generate the price equivalent. You confirm the value (Kg and price equivalent)

4

GET PAID

The price equivalent of the materials gets disbursed into your inCycle wallet immediately and can be transferred to your bank account when it exceeds the minimum threshold otherwise purchase airtime directly
